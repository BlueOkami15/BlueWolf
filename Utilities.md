`5s`

`True`

#Calculate
>
###Description:
Returns the **result of the expression** passed. **Keep in mind** `*` is used for multiplying. **Cannot** solve for variables.
###Aliases:
`calc, math`
###Cooldown:
`10s`  
###Delete On Use:  
`False`
###Works in DM's:
`True`  
###Usage: 
`calculate [number]<current units> to <desired units> - Convert between units`  
`calculate [calculation]`
